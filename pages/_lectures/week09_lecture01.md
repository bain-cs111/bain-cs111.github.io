Today we're introducing our last big concept of the quarter: the dictionary. It's another way (in addition to lists and tuples) of storing, representing, and analyzing collections of related data. As a programmer, you will encounter dictionaries all of the time. We'll go over some common scenarios and take a look at many different kinds of dictionaries.
